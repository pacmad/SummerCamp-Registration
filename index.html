Welcome! Happy Days Summer Camp at Outpost is a San Diego tradition and we look forward to you joining our camp family.It's not too early to start planning for summer.We challenge campers to develop a sense of group participation and individual stewardship, instilling confidence in each camper in a fun-filled atmosphere. we have been running quality camps that take kids of all ages to some of the most beautiful wilderness areas.Summer camps grows individual confidence, group cooperation, outdoor skills and environmental responsibility among its participants. Our safe reliable door-to-door transportation is a favorite of both campers and their parents!

Our directors are experienced professionals committed to the development of each child in our care, and work hard year-round to create our high-quality programs for our satisfied and happy parents. Welcome aboard!
